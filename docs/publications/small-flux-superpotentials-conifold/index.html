Small Flux Superpotentials for Type IIB Flux Vacua Close to a Conifold

with Ralph Blumenhagen, Max Brinkmann and Lorenz Schlechter

Abstract

We generalize the recently proposed mechanism by Demirtas, Kim, McAllister and Moritz arXiv:1912.10047 for the explicit construction of type IIB flux vacua with ∣W0∣≪1|W_0|\ll 1∣W0​∣≪1 to the region close to the conifold locus in the complex structure moduli space. For that purpose tools are developed to determine the periods and the resulting prepotential close to such a codimension one locus with all the remaining moduli still in the large complex structure regime. As a proof of principle we present a working example for the Calabi-Yau manifold P1,1,2,8,12[24]\mathbb{P}_{1,1,2,8,12}[24]P1,1,2,8,12​[24].
